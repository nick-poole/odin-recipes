Spaghetti In There

Hungry? There's spaghetti in there!

This one hits way too close to home. Growing up, your mom would make a whole pot of spaghetti on a weeknight after school. You love this spaghetti! It's your Mom's, after all, and it might even be safe to say that you have consumed this one dish more than any other in your entire life. With a piece of garlic toast and some parmesan cheese, you have a world class home cooked meal!

Homemade spaghetti is typically made in abundance which means there is usually a good amount left over for consumption the following day(s). So if you ever get hungry, there's spaghetti in there!

In a recent study, nutritionists have discovered that a single pot of Mom's spaghetti can feed a household of 4 for over a week. This phenomenon makes perfect sense when factoring in supply side economics, the law of conservation of leftovers, and the depletion ratio.

It does not matter how long it has been since it was made or how many times you've eaten it. Mom is not cooking when she gets home and she's not stopping at the drive thru. There's spaghetti in there and as God as my witness, you're going to eat it.

What you'll need

  • A microwave

Instructions

  1. This recipe need not be prepared as it has already been made. That's right! You know where it is and how to heat it up.
