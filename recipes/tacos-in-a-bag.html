Tacos in a Bag

Before we begin, I just want to acknowledge that, yes, this is exactly what this looks like. But hear me out. Suppose it tuesday, and by tuesday I mean Taco tuesday. After work, you stop by the store to grab taco ingredients. You know, the usual - ground beef, low fat sour cream, shredded cheese, etc. You make it all the way home before you realize that the taco kit you bought didn’t (for whatever reason) include taco shells or tortillas. I'm gonna tell you what you are not going to do. You aren’t getting back in your ‘03 Honda Civic and driving all the way to the store for taco shells. That’s madness and madness is not what taco Tuesday is all about.

Don't worry. We've got you covered.

This recipe takes the traditional approach to taco tuesday and incorporates a unique twist. It is perfect for casual social events or any taco sitaution that lacks shells or tortillas. If you have taco ingredients and a variety pack of mini Dorito ™,(or Fritos ® - we don't judge) then you have all you need for this tasty occasion.

What you'll need

  • 1 large skillet
  • Strainer or colander
  • 1 variety pack of Doritos ™
  • A can-do attitude

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup water
  • Ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • Chopped onions

    • Toppings

  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Mexican or taco cheese blend
  • Sour cream

    • Optional

  • Guacamole
  • Salsa
  • Sliced Jalapenos

Instructions

  1. First, you must believe. Because, before you can achieve, you must believe.

    2. Prepare the meat!

  2. Brown your onions adding minced garlic halfway through. Add ground beef and stir until properly browned.
  3. Drain excess fat and return to pan.
  4. Add water and taco seasoning to the pan and simmer uncovered for 3 to 4 minutes.

    5. Make the bags!

  5. Set out all your toppings buffet style on a counter or table.
  6. Open each individual bag and crush slightly.
  7. Spoon in taco meat and add toppings.
  8. Enjoy with a fork directly from the bag.
