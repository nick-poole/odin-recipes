Ingredients

Pasta

  • 1 pound ziti Pasta
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup italian cheese
  • 1/2 cup romano
  • 1/2 cup parmesean
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 pinch of italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Prepare ziti pasta according to package directions.
  2. In a medium pot, combine marina, tomatoes, sugar, and heavy cream.
  3. Bring to a simmer and add cheese. Stir until cheese melts
  4. Add drained pasta into sauce mix. Stir, add saand serve immediately
