Five Cheese Ziti

While I am a huge fan of free salad and breadsticks, I am not a huge fan of Olive Garden ™. However, this Olive Garden ™ copycat recipe is just too easy and too budget friendly not to be in your repertoire of weeknight quick fixes. If you play your cards right, you could spend $15 dollars or less making this dish. In 30 minutes or less, you will have an abundance of savory pasta perfection with plenty left over for lunch tomorrow. So crank that stove up high, boil that water, and throw in a pinch of salt. Now you're cooking with gas!

Ingredients

Pasta

  • 1 pound ziti Pasta
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup italian cheese
  • 1/2 cup fontina cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Topping

  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesean cheese
  • 1 pinch of italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Prepare ziti pasta according to package directions and drain. Be sure to save a small amount of that starchy pasta water for later use.
  2. In a medium pot, combine marina, tomatoes, sugar, and heavy cream.
  3. Bring to a simmer and stir in saved pasta water.
  4. Add fontina, ricotta, and italian cheeses to the mix. Stir until cheese melts.
  5. Add drained pasta into sauce mix and stir.
  6. Add remaining cheese and seasoning topping to dish and serve immediately.
